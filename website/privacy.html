Privacy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Local receipt data

POS Printer Emulator processes receipt jobs, parsed printer commands, and receipt history on the Windows computer where the software is installed. Receipt text, raw ESC/POS data, barcodes, QR-code contents, and rendered receipt images are never sent to our servers.

Installation, license, maintenance, and usage data

To operate the licensing and maintenance service, the application sends an installation identifier, customer or company name, email address, application version, active license level, license identifier when activated, maintenance status and coverage date, application-launch counts, emulated print-job counts, and last-seen time to POS Printer Emulator over an encrypted HTTPS connection. These records help us manage trials, active licenses, maintenance eligibility, product versions, and aggregate product usage.

Approximate geographic analytics

When you start a website download or the installed application registers or periodically reports usage, the public IP address associated with that request may be processed transiently to estimate a country and, for United States requests, a state. When our hosting provider does not supply that coarse location, the IP address is sent over HTTPS to an IP-geolocation service solely to perform the lookup. EPCOM Ltd. stores only the resulting country code, optional U.S. state code, and lookup time with download or installation usage records; it does not store the public IP address in the product-analytics database.

The private, two-factor-protected administration dashboard uses these coarse totals to show where downloads and product activity occur. Download totals record download starts and do not prove that an installer finished downloading or was installed. IP-derived geography is approximate and may be wrong because of VPNs, mobile carriers, corporate gateways, shared networks, or routing. It is not a customer address, is not used for advertising, and is not sold as location data.

The installation identifier and authentication token are randomly generated for this application installation. We do not use this information for advertising and do not sell it. Uninstalling the application stops future reporting. To request access, correction, or deletion of registration and usage records, contact us using the address below.

Purchase and renewal records

License purchases and optional maintenance renewals retain the customer details, license tier, amount, currency, payment-provider references, coverage dates, and fulfillment status needed to confirm payment and deliver the applicable activation or maintenance entitlement. Payment-card details are processed by PayPal and do not reach this website.

Website data

This website does not include advertising trackers or third-party analytics scripts. A download-start record includes the release version, referring site area, date, and coarse country or U.S. state derived as described above. The hosting provider may separately retain ordinary server logs such as IP address, requested page, browser type, and request time for security and reliability under its own retention and security practices.

Software activation

Activation keys and maintenance entitlement tokens are validated locally in the application. Registration details must match the information used to issue the activation key. Successful activation or maintenance renewal updates the installation's license and coverage status in the licensing dashboard without uploading receipt content.
