How licensing works

  1. Download and install the same application used for all license levels.
  2. Evaluate it in Trial Mode with up to five print jobs per day.
  3. Choose a Pro or Enterprise license through the secure purchase page. The current price for each level is shown at checkout.
  4. Receive a unique activation key for your customer registration.
  5. Enter the key in Settings to unlock the purchased license level immediately.

No separate paid-version download

Your existing installation unlocks its Pro or Enterprise features after successful validation. Your settings remain in place, so you can continue testing without reinstalling or reconfiguring the listener.

Licenses are issued for the registered customer or company. Keep your activation key private and contact support if you need help moving an installation.
