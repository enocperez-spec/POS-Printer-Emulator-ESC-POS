Lite, Pro, and Enterprise permanently include unlimited jobs, local history, watermark-free previews, exports, printer profiles, Stored Logos, Printer State, and capture/import/replay. Pro adds management for a second total listener; Enterprise expands the same isolated-listener workflow to 15 total listeners.

How licensing works

  1. Download and install the same application used for all license levels.
  2. Evaluate it in Trial Mode with up to five print jobs per day.
  3. Choose Lite, Pro, or Enterprise. Lite is $24.99; current Pro and Enterprise prices are administered centrally and shown on the secure Buy page.
  4. Receive a unique activation key for your customer registration.
  5. Enter the key in Settings to unlock the purchased license level immediately.

No separate paid-version download

Your existing installation unlocks its Lite, Pro, or Enterprise level after successful validation. Your settings remain in place, so you can continue testing without reinstalling or reconfiguring the listener.

Application Maintenance and Support

Beginning with v0.3.26, every new Lite, Pro, or Enterprise purchase includes 12 months of application updates, upgrades, assisted technical support, and access to Check for Updates. The underlying license remains permanent.

After maintenance expires, the purchased application and all existing licensed features continue working. Update checks, update downloads, and assisted support pause until an optional annual renewal is purchased. Local troubleshooting, health information, activation diagnostics, and privacy-safe log export remain available.

Renew early to add 12 months to the existing coverage end date. If coverage has expired, the renewed year begins on the confirmed payment date and restores eligible update and assisted-support access immediately. Existing paid customers received grandfathered maintenance through July 19, 2027 with the v0.3.26 release.

No subscription: neither the permanent license nor maintenance renews automatically. Each optional annual maintenance renewal is a separate, one-time purchase.
